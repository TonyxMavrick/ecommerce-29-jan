Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

At StyleNest, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order for any reason, we're here to help. Please read our return and refund policy carefully to understand your rights and our process.

1. Return Policy Overview

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt)

2. Eligibility for Returns

Items Eligible for Return

  • Clothing (shirts, pants, dresses, jackets, etc.)
  • Footwear (shoes, boots, sneakers) - must be unworn with original box
  • Accessories (bags, belts, scarves) - must be unused
  • Items purchased at full price or on sale

Items NOT Eligible for Return

  • Underwear, lingerie, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Gift cards
  • Items damaged due to misuse or normal wear and tear
  • Items without original tags or packaging

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please follow these steps:

Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Email us at care@stylenest.in or call +1-728-009-8383 with your order number and reason for return
  2. Get Authorization: We'll provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and return shipping instructions
  3. Pack Securely: Place the item(s) in original packaging with all tags attached
  4. Include Documentation: Add a copy of your order confirmation and the RA number
  5. Ship Back: Send the package to the address we provide

4. Return Shipping

Who Pays for Return Shipping?

  • Defective or Wrong Items: We cover all return shipping costs if you received a defective, damaged, or incorrect item
  • Change of Mind: You are responsible for return shipping costs if you're returning an item because you changed your mind, ordered the wrong size, or for any other non-defect reason

Return Shipping Address

Please do not ship items back without contacting us first. We will provide you with the correct return address and shipping label (if applicable) when you request a return authorization.

Important: We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance. We are not responsible for items lost or damaged in transit during the return process.

5. Refund Process

Processing Time

Once we receive your returned item, we will:

  1. Inspect the item within 2-3 business days
  2. Send confirmation email once the return is approved
  3. Process your refund within 5-7 business days

Refund Method

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds will be issued via:

  • Bank transfer to your provided account (fastest method)
  • Check mailed to your registered address
  • Store credit (instant, can be used immediately)

Please allow 5-10 business days for bank transfers to reflect in your account after we process the refund.

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, only partial refunds may be granted:

  • Items not in original condition, damaged, or missing parts not due to our error
  • Items returned more than 30 days after delivery
  • Items showing signs of wear or use

6. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size or color:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Place a new order for the item you want

Fast Exchange Option: To avoid waiting for the refund, you can place a new order immediately. Once we receive and process your return, we'll refund the original purchase.

7. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately (within 48 hours of delivery):

  • Email photos of the defect/damage to care@stylenest.in
  • Include your order number and description of the issue
  • We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you

For defective items, we will cover all return shipping costs and send a replacement via expedited shipping at no additional charge.

8. Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, we sincerely apologize for the error. Please:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the item you received
  • Do not use or remove tags from the incorrect item

We will send you the correct item immediately and arrange for pickup or provide a prepaid return label for the wrong item at no cost to you.

9. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the stated timeframe:

  1. Check your bank account or payment method again
  2. Contact your bank - there may be processing delays
  3. Contact us at care@stylenest.in with your order number and return tracking information

10. Sale Items

Items purchased during sales or promotions are eligible for return under the same conditions as regular-priced items, unless specifically marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" at the time of purchase.

Refunds for sale items will be issued at the price you paid, not the original price.

11. Gift Returns

If you received an item as a gift:

  • The item can be returned following our standard return policy
  • The refund will be issued to the original purchaser's payment method
  • Alternatively, we can issue a store credit to you in the amount of the purchase

Please contact us with the gift receipt or order number to process a gift return.

12. International Returns

Currently, we only ship within the United States. If you're located outside the US and received an item through a forwarding service, please note:

  • Returns must be shipped to our US address
  • You are responsible for all international return shipping costs
  • We cannot refund original shipping charges or customs fees
  • Return shipping times may be longer

13. Restocking Fees

StyleNest does not charge restocking fees for standard returns. However, we reserve the right to charge a restocking fee for:

  • Large or bulk orders
  • Items returned outside the 30-day return window
  • Items returned without proper authorization

14. Tips for Successful Returns

  • Keep tags attached: Don't remove tags until you're sure you want to keep the item
  • Save packaging: Keep the original box and packaging for at least 30 days
  • Try on carefully: Try items on over clothing to avoid makeup or deodorant stains
  • Document condition: Take photos when you receive items to document their condition
  • Contact us first: Always contact us before sending items back
